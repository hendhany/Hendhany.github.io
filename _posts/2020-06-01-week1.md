As a part of the research plan, I need spend the first weeks on reading research papers and articls to learn about different tool available to help student with learning database. On week one I studied the "SQL tester": This research paper about an online assesement tool for teaching and assessing SQL. Testing of basic SQL query language knowledge is completed with the SQL Tester as tested by Kleerekoper, et al. Students take practice tests and are allowed to revise their answers in a subsequent practice test. The output of SQL Tester illustrates four windows: 1) question, 2), database tables, 3) output from your query, and 4) desired output which allows the user to see results from the executed query. TriQL is similar in that it shows the query, the results from the query and the table schema.
